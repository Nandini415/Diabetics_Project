Our Mission

Diabetes, a condition where the body struggles with insulin production or usage, presents unique challenges for women. Hormonal changes, especially during pregnancy, can lead to gestational diabetes, which might evolve into type 2 diabetes. Additionally, conditions like PCOS increase diabetes risk. Fluctuations during menstrual cycles can also complicate diabetes management. Early detection is vital, so symptoms like frequent urination and constant fatigue shouldn't be overlooked. Fortunately, with a balanced diet and regular check-ups, diabetes is manageable. Awareness and proactive care are key, ensuring a healthier future for women and their families. Knowledge remains our best defense against this condition.
